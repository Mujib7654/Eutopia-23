Rules

There can be a minimum of 2 singers from a group. The maximum participants in a group should not exceed more than 4 members. Any instrument is allowed, except drums. You will be simply marked on your composition and lyrics.

A. Maximum of 4 members in a group is allowed(schools and colleges inclusive).
B. Drums are not allowed.
C. 2+3 Minutes for a group and 3 minutes for a solo.
D. No discrimination to any religion , no vulgar languages to be used in the lyrics.
E. Report to the venue at least 15 minutes prior to the mentioned time.
F. No instruments will be provided by the University.
