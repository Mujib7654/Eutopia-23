Introduction

Poetry according to Wordsworth was the spontaneous overflow of feelings recollected in tranquillity.
From Parnassian poets to Metaphysical poets, from Romanticism to Modernism, poetry has in itself evolved with us to fit our perspectives and identities.
Eutopia is giving you the opportunity to explore your incongruous imagery, through poetry, and a chance to discover self-expression through sonnets and odes.

Rules

  • ● The participants would be asked to submit a soft copy of their work prior to the event. To prevent any sort of plagiarism, they will be asked to bring a hard copy of the same on the day of the event.
    ● The first round of the competition will be open to any genre of the participants’ choice. The participants will be asked to recite their original compositions without any reference to the hard or soft copy of the same.
    ● Then the competition will move into its second round without any elimination.
    ● The second round of the event will be limited to poetry based on metaphors. The participants will be asked to pick chits that contain one metaphor each, randomly out of a bowl. Whatever metaphor they do get, will be what they have to base their poetry on.
    ● The participants will then be asked to recite their poetry.
    ● They will be judged and marked based on-

  • 1. Originality of content 2. Quality of content 3. Quality of recitation
