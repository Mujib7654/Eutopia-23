Introduction

The good thing about literature has always been its limitlessness regarding emotions whether it is Captain Haddock’s "Thousands of Blue Blistering Barnacles" or Gabbar Singh’s iconic, "Ye haat mujhe de de Thakur." How would it look if one of them said that out loud in Shinchan’s iconic voice? Literature is all about testing your imagination and discovering the world created by an author in a creative image.
Eutopia brings you Mood Board to explore different characters from newer and diverse perspectives and dive into your limits of imagination and enactments.

Rules

  • Participants would be asked to draw chits in two rounds.
    The first round would give them a random character accent out of 4 different distinct ones.
    The second draw would consist of 3 boxes labeled English, Bengali, and Hindi.
    Each box would contain chits with characters from popular literary or cinematic characters from the respective language genres.
    Brief character descriptions would be prepared in advance and given to the participants if required, to avoid any sort of miscommunication.
    Each character would have a short scene or panel assigned prior to the event. The task for the participants in the event will be to enact that scene in the accent that they have drawn in the first round.
    The participants would be judged and marked upon their ability to- 1. Enact the scene they have received.
    2. Accentuate the scene in the accent they have received.
