Introduction

The power of one's rationalization is a contributing factor towards their cognitive and intellectual wit. What better way to enhance these skills than to battle it out in Crusade Of Conceptions, a multilingual debate competition organized by Eutopia? This event is designed to allow you to audaciously put forward your unapologetic opinions, for we all know the only way to find your voice is to use it.

Rules

  • There would be two groups:
    Group A: School students from classes 8 -12
    Group B: Open to All (Students from undergraduate, post graduate, working professionals all can participate) Event Structure:
    The debate will be organized in 4 languages simultaneously viz. English, Hindi, Urdu and Bengali. Students can participate in whichever language they are comfortable with.
    The debate will be divided into two groups:
    Group A: School students from classes 9-12
    Group B: Open to All (Students from undergraduate, post graduate, working professionals all can participate)
    Rules and Regulations:
    No. of participants :
    Group A: All schools can send 1-3 teams per language. 1 team will consist of two speakers (One going for the motion and other against it.)
    Group B: Participants can register individually or in groups. People will be selected on a first come first basis.
    1) Time: Speakers will get 3+1 Minutes to present their views and arguments, followed by 1 minute of Rebuttal.
    2) Speakers should either be in uniform or maintain a formal dress code.
    3) Decisions of the Judges shall be final and obligatory.
